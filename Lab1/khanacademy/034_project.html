Spaghetti Carbonara

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Spaghetti 400g
Eggs 4
Parmesan Cheese 100g, grated
Pancetta 150g
Garlic 2 cloves
Black Pepper To taste

Step 1: Boil a large pot of salted water. Cook the spaghetti until al dente.

Step 2: In a bowl, whisk the eggs and mix in the grated Parmesan cheese.

Step 3: In a pan, cook the pancetta until crisp. Add minced garlic and cook for 1 minute.

Step 4: Drain the spaghetti and immediately mix it with the pancetta. Remove from heat and quickly add the egg mixture, tossing to create a creamy sauce. Add black pepper to taste.

Step 5: Serve immediately with extra Parmesan cheese on top.

Chicken Alfredo

  • Time: 25 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Fettuccine 400g
Chicken Breast 2, boneless and skinless
Butter 100g
Heavy Cream 1 cup
Parmesan Cheese 100g, grated
Garlic 2 cloves, minced
Salt To taste
Black Pepper To taste

Step 1: Cook the fettuccine according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.

Step 2: In a large skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and cook for 1 minute.

Step 3: Add the chicken breasts and cook until golden brown and cooked through. Remove from the skillet and slice into strips.

Step 4: In the same skillet, add the heavy cream and bring to a simmer. Stir in the grated Parmesan cheese until melted and smooth.

Step 5: Return the sliced chicken to the skillet and toss to coat in the sauce. Add the cooked fettuccine and toss to combine. Season with salt and black pepper to taste.

Step 6: Serve immediately with extra Parmesan cheese on top.
